Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: August 12, 2025

Overview

This page provides instructions for users who want to delete their data from AI Live Commerce. We respect your privacy and provide multiple ways for you to request deletion of your personal information.

Once your data is deleted, this action cannot be undone. You will lose access to all your AI Live Commerce content, settings, and connected Facebook permissions.

What Data We Store

AI Live Commerce may store the following information:

  • Facebook profile information (name, email, profile picture)
  • Facebook Page information (if you're a Page administrator)
  • AI-generated scripts and content
  • Audio files generated by our text-to-speech service
  • Usage analytics and logs
  • Account preferences and settings

Data Deletion Methods

Method 1: Self-Service Account Deletion

Steps:

  1. Log into your AI Live Commerce account
  2. Go to Account Settings
  3. Click on "Privacy & Data"
  4. Select "Delete My Account"
  5. Confirm deletion by typing "DELETE" in the confirmation box
  6. Click "Permanently Delete Account"

Processing Time: Immediate for most data, up to 30 days for backup systems

Method 2: Email Request

Steps:

  1. Send an email to: supwin@gmail.com
  2. Subject: "Data Deletion Request - AI Live Commerce"
  3. Include your Facebook email address or User ID
  4. Specify what data you want deleted (or "all data")
  5. We will respond within 72 hours

Processing Time: 5-30 business days

Method 3: Facebook Data Deletion Callback

When you remove AI Live Commerce from your Facebook apps, we automatically receive a deletion request and will delete your data within 30 days.

Steps:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings & Privacy ‚Üí Settings
  2. Click "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find "AI Live Commerce"
  4. Click "Remove"
  5. Confirm removal

Processing Time: Automatic, completed within 30 days

What Happens When Data is Deleted

Immediate Deletion (within 24 hours):

  • Account access is revoked
  • Facebook app permissions are removed
  • Personal profile information is deleted
  • AI-generated content is removed

Complete Deletion (within 30 days):

  • All data removed from backup systems
  • Analytics data anonymized or deleted
  • Log files purged
  • Audio files permanently deleted

Data Retention Exceptions

We may retain some data longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Legal compliance: Data required by law (tax records, etc.)
  • Security: Security logs for fraud prevention (anonymized, 90 days)
  • Business records: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data
  • Active disputes: Data related to ongoing legal matters

Verification Process

To protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized deletion requests, we may ask you to verify your identity by:

  • Logging into your account
  • Providing your registered email address
  • Answering security questions
  • Confirming Facebook account ownership

Partial Data Deletion

You can also request deletion of specific types of data:

  • Audio files only: Delete generated audio while keeping account
  • Scripts only: Delete AI-generated scripts while keeping settings
  • Facebook data only: Remove Facebook integration data
  • Analytics data only: Remove usage tracking data

Troubleshooting

Common Issues:

  • Can't access account: Use Method 2 (Email Request)
  • Forgot email address: Contact support with Facebook profile URL
  • Deletion not working: Clear browser cache and try again
  • Still receiving emails: Check if deletion is complete (may take 30 days)

Data Download Before Deletion

Before deleting your data, you can download a copy:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Account Settings ‚Üí Data Export
  3. Select data types to download
  4. Click "Request Export"
  5. Download will be available within 24 hours
